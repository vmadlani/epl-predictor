Usage rules

38 mark Always #E8521C. Never outlined, never on a coloured background other than black.
Wordmark weight "Thirty" is always 300 (light), "Eight" is always 700 (bold). Never reverse these.
Page backgrounds White (#FFFFFF) for content, black (#111) for the nav only. No gradients.

Tagline "Premier League predictions, model-driven." Use in sub-headings and social profiles.
Name rationale
Why ThirtyEight? A Premier League season is 38 gameweeks. Every number on this site — every projected points total, every title probability, every relegation percentage — is the product of reasoning about all 38. The name follows the same logic as FiveThirtyEight: a specific, meaningful number that only makes sense once you understand the subject, and that sounds unexpectedly cool to everyone else.
